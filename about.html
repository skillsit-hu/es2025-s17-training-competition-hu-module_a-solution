Our Story

Founded in 2019 by a team of educators and technologists, SkillShare Academy emerged from a simple observation: traditional education wasn't keeping pace with the rapidly evolving job market.

Our founders, having witnessed talented individuals struggle to access quality professional development, set out to create a platform that would bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 15,000 professionals across 50 countries advance their careers through our innovative combination of expert-led courses and personalized mentorship.
